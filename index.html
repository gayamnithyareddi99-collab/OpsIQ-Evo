FAQ

Common Questions

What is Agentic AI and how is it different from a normal dashboard?

+
Traditional inventory dashboards only report data they show you there's a problem. OpsIQ Evo's Agentic AI is an "Acting Agent" it doesn't just flag a stockout risk; it autonomously scouts alternative suppliers, verifies their credentials via blockchain, drafts a contract, and presents you with 3 pre-verified options (Lowest Cost, Lowest Carbon, Highest Speed) for one-tap approval. This is the fundamental shift from passive monitoring to active resilience.

How does the Regulatory Shield handle the Simpler Recycling Act 2025?

+
The Regulatory Shield uses a specialised NLP module trained exclusively on UK environmental law, including the Simpler Recycling Act 2025 (mandatory food waste separation for businesses with 10+ employees from March 31, 2025). It automatically classifies waste streams, tracks volumes in real time, generates audit-ready council reports, and verifies supplier ESG certificates automating over 90% of mandatory reporting and protecting your business from municipal fines.

Do I need a technical team or IT department to use OpsIQ Evo?

+
No technical expertise is required. The mobile-first dashboard is built for busy store managers, not data scientists. Integration with existing POS systems, spreadsheets, and supplier portals is handled via RESTful APIs with pre-built connectors. The platform goes live within 48 hours. The offline-first Edge AI also means it works reliably in basement stockrooms or venues with poor connectivity no constant internet dependency needed.

What is the expected ROI for an SME deploying OpsIQ Evo?

+
Based on our pilot data and operational benchmarks, SMEs can expect a 94% reduction in stockouts, 70% reduction in back-office procurement time, and full regulatory audit-readiness under the 2025 Simpler Recycling Act. At £49/month for the Basic tier, businesses recover the subscription cost within days through avoided waste, prevented fines (which can reach thousands of pounds for non-compliance), and optimised procurement achieving measurable ROI in the first month of operation.
